Welcome to the Crop Prediction System

SOur Crop Prediction System is designed to assist farmers in making informed decisions about their crop choices based on environmental factors such as soil pH, rainfall, and geographical location. By leveraging advanced data analytics and machine learning algorithms, the system predicts suitable crops that are likely to thrive in your specified conditions.


Why Use Crop Prediction?
Optimized Yield:

Reduce risks associated with planting unsuitable crops, such as poor growth or crop failure.

Allocate resources more efficiently by matching crops to environmental conditions.
